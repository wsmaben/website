Calendar of Events

Below is a calendar of WSMA events and WSMA-sponsored or accredited events. The calendar also includes state specialty and county medical society events. Click on the event title for more information about the event. Click on the 'agenda' tab for a chronological list of all events.

For more CME opportunities, review our list of WSMA-accredited CME providers under Resources to find the provider nearest to you and to check their current CME offerings.

Events are color coded as follows:

  • blue - General WSMA events and meetings of WSMA's standing committees. Includes some events supported by the WSMA.
  • red - WSMA officers' schedule
  • green - WSMA-sponsored educational events, WSMA jointly-provided CME*
  • yellow - State specialty society events
  • light blue - County medical society events

*With CompleteCME™ the WSMA will work with you to award CME credit for your activities.

Selecting the event, then clicking on 'copy to my calendar' will allow you to add the event to your personal calendar, provided you have a Google account (signing up is free).

If you're a state specialty or county medical society and would like to have your event added to our calendar, email event information to Abby Vincent at abby@wsma.org.
