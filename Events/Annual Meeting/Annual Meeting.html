WSMA Annual Meeting

The 2020 WSMA Annual Meeting is scheduled for Sept. 26-27 at the Historic Davenport, Autograph Collection in Spokane. Save the dates!

The Annual Meeting of the WSMA House of Delegates is the WSMA's premiere policy-making event. All WSMA members are encouraged to attend, to help inform policymaking, take advantage of educational offerings, and network with peers and colleagues from throughout the state. Learn more about the meeting below.

Physicians interested in how the WSMA serves the profession are also encouraged to attend, to learn more about how association sets policy that drives our advocacy on behalf of the house of medicine.

For the Official Actions of the 2019 WSMA House of Delegates, visit the House of Delegates page. WSMA members interested in serving as a delegate at the 2020 Annual Meeting, contact slb@wsma.org.

Thank you for attending the 2019 WSMA Annual Meeting!

EXCLUSIVE PREMIER PARTNER

Founded in 1981 under the auspices of the WSMA, Physicians Insurance A Mutual Company is the Northwest's leading medical professional liability insurance company, physician-owned and exclusively endorsed to this day by the WSMA. For over 30 years, Physicians Insurance has remained the stable, committed and local insurer for physicians, clinics, facilities and hospitals, providing risk-management consulting, continuing medical education and claims administration services to more than 7,000 members in Washington, Idaho, Oregon and Wyoming.

SILVER PARTNERS

Premera Blue Cross' mission is to provide peace of mind to our members about their healthcare. We provide health insurance and related services to 1.9 million people in Washington and Alaska. Premera Blue Cross has operated in Washington since 1933 and Alaska since 1952 and is an independent licensee of the Blue Cross Blue Shield Association.
