Membership in the WSMA is available to physicians, physician assistants, residents/fellows and medical students. Membership dues and requirements for each are detailed on the links to your left.

Physicians must pay their WSMA dues directly through their county medical society in the following counties:

  • Kitsap
  • Whatcom

Physicians in the following unified counties, must belong to their county medical society in order to be a member of the WSMA:

  • Kittitas
  • Okanogan
  • Thurston-Mason
  • Walla Walla
  • Yakima

WSMA dues include a $25 contribution for the Washington State Medical Political Action Committee (WAMPAC) or WSMA Foundation for Health Care Improvement, a not-for-profit 501c3 organization which supports improvements in the quality and delivery of health care services for all Washingtonians. When paying your dues, please select WAMPAC or WSMA Foundation to indicate where you'd like your contribution directed.

If you need assistance, please contact member services at (206) 441-9762 M-F 9 a.m. - 5 p.m. or email membership@wsma.org.
