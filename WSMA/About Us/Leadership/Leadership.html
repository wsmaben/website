Leadership

Crucial to the WSMA's success as our state's premier physician professional association is a demographically representative volunteer leadership. The WSMA works to identify, nurture and renew our leadership on a yearly basis. For more information about service as a WSMA officer, trustee or delegate, contact Shannon Bozarth, 206.441.9762 or slb@wsma.org.

House of Delegates

All legislative power of the WSMA is vested in the approximately 200-member House of Delegates, which meets yearly.
