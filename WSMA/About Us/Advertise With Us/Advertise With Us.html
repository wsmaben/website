How to place your ad

To advertise, contact Milana McLead at 206.956.3629 or milana@wsma.org. Ads must be received by the first of the week prior to publication. The Membership Memo is published the 2nd and 4th week of each month. Advertisers may cancel orders up to two weeks prior to the Membership Memo publication date. Display ads are placed on a variety of the most popular pages on www.wsma.org. A single ad will appear on every page in the legal guide. Payment is required prior to ad placement. We accept check, Visa or MasterCard.

Advertising is subject to space availability. WSMA reserves the right to approve all advertising.

Print Display Advertising

Print display advertising will appear in our WSMA Reports, 4/C, 16- 20-page newsletter printed six times per year and mailed to almost 11,000 members.

Print display ad sizes

Full page (image area, without bleed): 7" wide by 11" high
Back cover (image area, without bleed): 7" wide by 9" high
Half page (image area): 7" wide by 4-5/8" high

Specs for printed display ads

PDF/X-1a formatted ads are preferred with all fonts and images embedded (no native files). Scanned images must be high resolution (300 dpi) and CMYK or grayscale (no spot colors, RGB, LAB, or ICC color profiles please). Please use 133-line screen for halftones and tints.
