Policies

The WSMA is governed by four policy documents: WSMA Policy Compendium, WSMA Principles of Medical Ethics, WSMA Bylaws and WSMA Judicial Council opinions.

If you have questions about WSMA's policy on a particular issue, please contact Jennifer Hanscom , WSMA's executive director/CEO.

WSMA Policy Compendium

The WSMA Policy Compendium collects all policy as passed by the WSMA House of Delegates. Review the compendium, available online .

WSMA Principles of Medical Ethics

The WSMA Principles of Medical Ethics are the current Principles of Medical Ethics of the American Medical Association . These principles outline the standards of conduct that define the essentials of honorable behavior for the physician.

WSMA Bylaws

Download the WSMA Bylaws , revised November 2019. These bylaws define the association, its governance and regulation of its affairs.

Opinion & Reports of the Judicial Council

Opinions of the WSMA Judicial Council reflect the association's position on a variety of medical ethical issues. Opinions of the Judicial Council are intended as guidelines to responsible professional behavior.
