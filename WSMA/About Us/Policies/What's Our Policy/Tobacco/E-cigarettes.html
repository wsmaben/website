E-cigarettes

The WSMA supports legislation that prohibits sales and marketing of e-cigarettes and similar devices to minors. (Res C-5, A-13)

The WSMA supports legislation that prohibits use of e-cigarettes and similar devices in public spaces. (Res C-5, A-13)

The WSMA advocates for measures to ban the sale of flavored e-cigarettes in Washington state (Res B-20, A-19)

The WSMA advocates for increased regulation of flavored liquid nicotine packaging to prohibit the marketing of e-cigarettes to children. (Res B-20, A-19)
