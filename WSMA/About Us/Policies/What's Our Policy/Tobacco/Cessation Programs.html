Cessation Programs

The WSMA encourages its members to strongly advise their smoking patients about tobacco cessation. (Res A-3, A-95) (Res A-4, A-14)

The WSMA has established a priority of preventing tobacco-related infant mortality and illness by endorsing and actively promoting the development of programs to assist pregnant women with smoking cessation programs. (Res A-4, A-95) (Res A-4, A-14)
