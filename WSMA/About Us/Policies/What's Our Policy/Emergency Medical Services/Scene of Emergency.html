Scene of Emergency

The WSMA supports every effort to enhance cooperation between physicians and paramedical emergency personnel. The WSMA urges all local medical societies to consider a policy regarding this matter, then publish and distribute a card delineating policy for use by local paramedics or individuals who are certified in advanced life support systems. (PD Rpt B, '82; PD Rpt B, '83) (Reaffirmed A-17)
