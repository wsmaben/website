Sexually Transmitted Infection (STI) Control

The WSMA encourages continued research on innovative strategies for sexually transmitted infection (STI) control, particularly in patients for whom Patient-Delivered Partner Therapy is not currently a well-accepted strategy. (Res A-2, A-04) (Reaffirmed A-17)
