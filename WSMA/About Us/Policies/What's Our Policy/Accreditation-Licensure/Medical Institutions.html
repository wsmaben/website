Medical Institutions

The WSMA urges the state of Washington to maintain appropriate accreditation for all its state medical institutions, and to expend the funds necessary to maintain standards set by accrediting authorities in order to maintain an acceptable level of psychiatric care. (Res 52, B-89) (Reaffirmed A-17)
