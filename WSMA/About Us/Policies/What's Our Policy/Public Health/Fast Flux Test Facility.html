Fast Flux Test Facility

The WSMA opposes restart of the Fast Flux Test Facility (FFTF) based on the current public health impact resulting from Hanford's existing storage facilities, and the potential for further adverse public health consequences as a result of restarting the FFTF reactor. (EC 1/98) (Reaffirmed A-17)
