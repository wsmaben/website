Racism

The WSMA affirms that racism in its systemic, structural, institutional, and interpersonal forms is an urgent threat to public health, the advancement of health equity, and a barrier to excellence in the delivery of medical care. (B-9, A-20)

WSMA affirms that racism, discrimination and hate crimes are a public health issue. (B-13, A-20)
