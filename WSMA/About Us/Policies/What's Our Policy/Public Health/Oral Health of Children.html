Oral Health of Children

The WSMA supports the identification and treatment of oral health problems in mothers and children by primary care physicians within the limits of their knowledge and skills by supporting education and advocacy efforts aimed at reducing the burden of disease in low-income and high-risk families. (Res A-5, A-02) (Reaffirmed A-17)
