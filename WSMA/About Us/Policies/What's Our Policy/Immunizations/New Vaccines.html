New Vaccines

The WSMA urges all physicians caring for children and adolescents to promote vaccines for children and adolescents and also use the immunization visits as opportunities to provide other important services, such as well child check-ups, risk assessment, and counseling, to this age group. (Res A-1, A-06) (Amended A-19)
