Hemophilus Influenza Type B Immunization

The WSMA supports the adoption of legislation to require that children under age 5 be immunized against Hemophilus Influenza Type B in order to attend public and private day care facilities and preschools. (Res 13, A-85) (Reaffirmed A-17)
