HPV

The WSMA urges physicians to follow the Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices (ACIP) recommendation for routine administration of human papilloma virus (HPV) vaccine. (Res A-1, A-06) (Amended A-19)

The WSMA supports state funding for HPV vaccine to the state immunization program, with state support for an adequate supply of vaccine. (Res A-1, A-06) (Amended A-19)
