Hepatitis A

The WSMA supports routine vaccination of children ages 2 to 18 against hepatitis A, and supports efforts to provide information about the safety, effectiveness and importance of the hepatitis A vaccination. (Res A-4, A-99) (Reaffirmed A-17)
