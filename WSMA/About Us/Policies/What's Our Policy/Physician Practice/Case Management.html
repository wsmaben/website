Case Management

The WSMA recognizes patient-physician and patient-care related telephone consultation and related physician case management ministrations as legitimate medical services requiring physician time and expertise and affirms the physicians' right to charge and receive payment for such consultation. (Res 6, B-91) (Amended A-17)
