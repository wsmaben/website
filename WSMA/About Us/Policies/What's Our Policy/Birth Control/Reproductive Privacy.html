Reproductive Privacy

The WSMA encourages reproductive privacy, including individual rights to choose or refuse the use of birth control. (EC 9/91; C-14, A-06) (Reaffirmed A-19)

The WSMA supports access to over-the-counter oral contraceptive pills (C-10, A-20)
