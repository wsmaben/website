Care Coverage

The WSMA supports the use of non-pharmacologic treatments when deemed appropriate to treat and manage acute and chronic pain. (Res C-2, A-18)

The WSMA supports requiring coverage for diabetes prevention, lifestyle modification programs, and community health workers in the state Medicaid Accountable Communities of Health and other commercial and public value-based payment systems. (Res C-18, A-18)
