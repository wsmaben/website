Donation

The WSMA supports the exploration of methods to greatly increase organ donation, including the “presumed consent” modality or organ donation for the state of Washington (Res C-5, A-98), and will work to educate its members and the public on the benefits of a presumed consent policy of organ donation. (Res A-6, A-99) (Reaffirmed A-17)
