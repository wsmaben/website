Tax on Alcohol

The WSMA supports legislation increasing an excise tax on alcoholic beverages, with the revenue dedicated to help support victims of alcohol abuse and educational efforts to prevent alcohol abuse. (Res 44, C-89) (Reaffirmed A-17)
