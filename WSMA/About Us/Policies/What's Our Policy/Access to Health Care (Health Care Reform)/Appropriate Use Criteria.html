Appropriate Use Criteria

The WSMA supports the use of evidence-based appropriate use criteria (AUC) by insurance companies as a requirement before payers can reject a claim and that imaging tests scored as "appropriate" by the AUC not be eligible for rejection by payers. (Res C-21 A-17));
