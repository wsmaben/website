Costs

Health care cost predictability to include the elimination of all cost-shifting, explicit rationing (basic benefits), technology review, the use of consistent and responsible utilization review, consumer education and cost sharing, and the use of data and marketplace dynamics (i.e. contracting mechanisms, negotiated rates, payment reform) by purchasers and providers of care.
