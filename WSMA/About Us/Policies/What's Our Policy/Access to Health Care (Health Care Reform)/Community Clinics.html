Community Clinics

The WSMA believes that maintaining community clinics is vital to medically indigent patients and their families who would not otherwise be served by the medical community. (Res A-8, A-94) (Reaffirmed A-17)

The WSMA recognizes the importance of community health clinics in providing care for some Medicaid patients and encourages that these clinics be adequately funded. (EC Rpt D, A-95) (Reaffirmed A-17)
