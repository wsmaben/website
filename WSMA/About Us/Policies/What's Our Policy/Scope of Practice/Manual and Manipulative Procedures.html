Manual and Manipulative Procedures

The WSMA supports the concept that manual and manipulative procedures are clearly within the scope of practice of practicing allopathic and osteopathic physicians and surgeons when such procedures are performed for purposes other than chiropractic. (Res B-1, A-97) (Reaffirmed A-17)
