Injured Workers

The WSMA supports changes to the applicable laws and regulations in Washington state relating to the treatment of injured workers to replace the concept of "fixed" and "stable" with the more appropriate concept of "maximum medical improvement". (Res B-5, A-97; EC Rpt E, A-98) (Reaffirmed A-17)

The WSMA supports policies that help injured workers receive cost-effective medical care. (Res C-23, A-18)
