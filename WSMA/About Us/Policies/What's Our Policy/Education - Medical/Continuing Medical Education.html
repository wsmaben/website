Continuing Medical Education

The WSMA opposes AMA policy revisions to the Physician Recognition Award which requires applicants to obtain a minimum of 20 hours of Category II credit each year, and disallows credit for independent study or reading. (Reaffirmed A-17)

The WSMA opposes legislation which would eliminate the current continuing medical education requirements. The WSMA supports mandating continuing medical education, but opposes disease specific mandatory education requirements. (EC 9/91; EC Rpt D, '91) (Reaffirmed A-17)
