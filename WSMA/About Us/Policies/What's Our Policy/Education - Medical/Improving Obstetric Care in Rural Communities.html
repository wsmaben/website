Improving Obstetric Care in Rural Communities

The WSMA support the creation and/or maintenance of surgical training programs for rural obstetrics providers, including continuing education through hospital partnerships, and rural family medicine residency programs. (B-6, A-20)

The WSMA support the formation of new obstetric fellowships, and expansion of current obstetric fellowships in Washington State (B-6, A-20)

The WSMA support the concept that members engaged in obstetrical practice should have affordable access, both financially and in their time allocation, to the CME that is needed to perform their obstetric duties well. (B-6, A-20)
