Evidence-Based Sex Education in Washington State Schools

The WSMA support implementing accurate, comprehensive, and evidence-based sexual education in Washington state middle and high schools. (Res C-9, A-19)

The WSMA encourage legislation that requires the inclusion of contraception options, condom use demonstrations, LGBTQ+ sexual health education, STI education, and discussions of consent in all Washington state sex education courses. (Res C-9, A-19)
