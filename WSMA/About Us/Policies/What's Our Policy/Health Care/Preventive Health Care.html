Preventive Health Care

The WSMA encourages physicians to offer evidence-based cost-effective preventive care and early detection tests and encourages all third-party payers to cover the cost of evidence-based cost-effective preventive care and early detection tests. (Res A-8, A-00; A-01) (Reaffirmed A-17)
