Medical Savings Accounts

The WSMA affirms its support for the goal of universal access to health insurance for all Washington citizens and recognizes and supports that Medical Savings Accounts (MSAs) should be given an opportunity to be further investigated, developed and implemented as one methodology to achieve this goal. (BT 1/95) (Reaffirmed A-17)
