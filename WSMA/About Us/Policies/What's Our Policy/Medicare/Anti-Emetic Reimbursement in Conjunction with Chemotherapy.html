Anti-Emetic Reimbursement in Conjunction with Chemotherapy

The WSMA supports the concept of anti-emetics and chemotherapy being considered as a single covered service and should continue to be covered as an integral part of chemotherapy. (Late Res 2, A-92) (Reaffirmed A-17)
