Payment Reform

The WSMA supports the formation of a single statewide Medicare physician payment region for the state of Washington. (Res 10, A-92; Res B-2, A-94) (Reaffirmed A-17)

The WSMA supports the elimination of discriminatory payment policies imposed on new physicians. (EC 2/92) (Reaffirmed A-17)

The WSMA supports changes in rules regarding billing for medical supplies to allow adequate reimbursement for costs. (EC 2/92) (Reaffirmed A-17)
