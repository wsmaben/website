Smart Guns

The WSMA supports broadening the mandate of the existing Office for Firearm Violence Prevention to: Develop proposals to incentivize firearm sellers in the state of Washington to offer “smart guns” for sale and to incentivize the purchase of “smart guns” in place of “conventional” guns (C-14, A-20).
