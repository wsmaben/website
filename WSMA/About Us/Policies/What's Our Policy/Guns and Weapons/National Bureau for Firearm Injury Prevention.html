National Bureau for Firearm Injury Prevention

The WSMA supports the creation of a National Bureau for Firearm Injury Prevention which would lead and coordinate a long term, multidisciplinary campaign to reduce firearm injury and deaths based on proven public health research and practices. (C-12, A-20)
