Safe-Injection Facilities

Our WSMA advocates for the development and implementation of pilot safe-injection facilities in Washington state that are designed, monitored and evaluated to generate data to inform policymakers on the feasibility, effectiveness and legal aspects of safe-injection facilities in reducing harms and health care costs related to injection drug use. (Res B-4 A-17)
