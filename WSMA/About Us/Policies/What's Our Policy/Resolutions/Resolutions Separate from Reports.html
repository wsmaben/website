Resolutions Separate from Reports

All policy matters brought before the WSMA House of Delegates for a vote be submitted in the form of a resolution, separate from committee reports, with supporting arguments presented in “Whereas” statements and proposed new policies presented s “Resolved” statements. (Res B-1, A-18)

The authors or groups introducing a resolution to the WSMA House of Delegates must be clearly identified. (Res B-1, A-18)

Committee reports are considered informational items and are designed to inform rather than change WSMA policy, with the exception of the Bylaws Committee reports, whose purpose is to propose amendments. (Res B-1, A18)
