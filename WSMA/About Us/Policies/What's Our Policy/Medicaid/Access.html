Access

In order to provide more adequate access to health care for Washington state's needy, the WSMA strongly supports increased funding for the state Medicaid program. (EC Rpt P, '88) (Reaffirmed A-17)

The WSMA supports reimbursement policies that promote access to the Medicaid program in accordance with its intent and enabling legislation. (Res 48, C-88) (Reaffirmed A-17)
