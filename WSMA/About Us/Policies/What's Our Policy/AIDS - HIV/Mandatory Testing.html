Mandatory Testing

WSMA opposes HIV testing as a condition of medical staff privileges. (EC Rpt H, A-92) (Reaffirmed A-17)

The WSMA opposes mandatory testing of health care professionals, as well as mandatory disclosure of HIV seropositive health care professionals. (EC 9/91) (Reaffirmed A-17)
