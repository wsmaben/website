The AMA

All WSMA members are strongly encouraged to become members of the AMA so that the voice of Washington state physicians can continue to be articulated to the fullest extent on the national level. (Res D-6, A-04) (Reaffirmed A-17)

Our WSMA supports funding for student representatives from each of the medical schools in Washington state, to be considered by the WSMA Executive Committee, to attend the annual and interim meetings of the American Medical Association Medical Student Section (Res B-2 A-17)
