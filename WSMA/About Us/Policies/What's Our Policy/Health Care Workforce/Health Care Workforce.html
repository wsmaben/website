Health Care Workforce

The WSMA supports strategies that will link the issues of economic development with early education career counseling and curriculum selection so as to attract necessary qualified applicants into medicine and associated health care careers. (EC Rpt P, '89; EC Rpt B, '90) (Reaffirmed A-17)
