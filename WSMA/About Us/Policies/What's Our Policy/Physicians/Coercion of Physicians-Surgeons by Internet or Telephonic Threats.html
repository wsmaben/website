Coercion of Physicians/Surgeons by Internet or Telephonic Threats

The WSMA oppose the coercion of physicians and surgeons by anyone who uses internet, telephonic, or other technology, and that the WSMA continue to provide resources and education to address issues of management of one’s online reputation. (Res C-1, A-19)
