Equity and Justice in Care and Outcomes of Washingtonians with Cancer

Equity and Justice in Care and Outcomes of Washingtonians with Cancer The WSMA acknowledges the existence of racial disparities in cancer care and outcome and is committed to reducing them. (Res B-6, A-19)

The WSMA commits to partnering with medical and advocacy communities to facilitate comprehensive public health solutions. (Res B-6, A-19)

RESOLVED, the WSMA supports sustained investments in high-quality screening and patient navigation involving high-quality referrals with the goal of empowering disadvantaged patients throughout the complex continuum of care. (Res B-6, A-19)
