Coverage of Low-Dose CT Scans

The WSMA supports the AMA's recommendation that coverage of screening low-dose CT (LDCT) scans for patients at high risk for lung cancer by Medicare, Medicaid and private insurance be a required covered benefit. (Res A-4, A-14)
