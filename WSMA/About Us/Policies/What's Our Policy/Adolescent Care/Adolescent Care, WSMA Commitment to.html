Adolescent Care, WSMA Commitment to

The WSMA is committed to Adolescent Health Care Issues through continued support of community awareness programs such as PACE and the WSMA Auxiliary's annual statewide Teen Health Forum, to address and educate teens about healthy lifestyles and the major adolescent morbidities including teen pregnancy, sexually transmitted diseases, substance abuse, and accident and injury prevention. (Reaffirmed A-17)
