CompleteCME® is a joint provider program that offers a full complement of services to meet your continuing medical education needs.


CompleteCME® Services

Full event coordination

The WSMA will handle all arrangements for your annual meeting or live event, including full-service event planning, grant and exhibitor coordination, and CME credit management.

CME oversight

The WSMA will work with you to award AMA PRA Category 1 Credit™ for your activities. We will train and support you through each step of the process, from the early planning stages through application submission to post-activity reporting.

Contact us if you are a non-accredited organization that would like to provide CME credit for any of the following:

Live activities

  • Annual meetings
  • Courses
  • Webinars

Regularly scheduled series

  • Case conferences
  • Grand Rounds
  • Cancer Conference Series
  • M & M’s

Enduring materials

  • Online Courses
