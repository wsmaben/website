COVID-19 Q&A Session - Feb. 24, 2021

With a focus on what front-line physicians need to know about the virus, the WSMA brings together Washington state's public health leaders and leading experts for free sessions that offer the latest information on COVID-19 that directly impacts you, your practice, and your patients. This members-only webinar series addresses the evolution of the disease, management of COVID-19 patients, vaccines, and our state's response to the pandemic.

An expert panel of physicians and public health professionals answers questions about COVID-19 testing, treatment options, long-term follow-up, and vaccines.

(Originally recorded on Feb. 24, 2021)
