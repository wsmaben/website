Opioids and Telehealth in a Pandemic

This one-hour Continuing Professional Development webinar will help physicians and advanced practitioners care for chronic pain patients with opioid prescriptions via telemedicine during the pandemic. The webinar covers: strategies to provide remote care for patients that you usually need to see in person, how to bill for telemedicine, regulatory changes to opioid prescribing and telemedicine policies, and the key features of the Better Prescribing, Better Treatment acute opioid reports.
