Successfully Navigating the Washington State MD/PA Licensure and Renewal Process: What You Need to Know



The Washington State Medical Association (WSMA) is hosting experts from the Washington Medical Commission’s licensing department to provide insight on the licensing and renewal process, share advice, and offer tips on how to get your application approved as quickly as possible. The webinar will cover utilization of the online portal, accessing the online application checklist, requesting documents through the original entity, and when and how to complete the fingerprinting process, among other important topics. There will also be an opportunity to have your licensing questions answered by the WMC representatives.

Guest Speakers

  • Marisa Courtney, licensing manager, Washington Medical Commission
  • Emma Marienthal, licensing lead, Washington Medical Commission
  • Jeb Shepard, policy director, Washington State Medical Association

(Originally recorded on April 16, 2021)
