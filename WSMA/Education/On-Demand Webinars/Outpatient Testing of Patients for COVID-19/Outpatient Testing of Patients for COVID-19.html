Outpatient Testing of Patients for COVID-19: Who, How, and When to Test and What to Do with the Results

COVID-19 Virtual Grand Rounds (Part 2)

Yuan-Po Tu, MD, from The Everett Clinic, outlines best-known-practices for pre-analytic, analytic, and post-analytic stages of COVID-19 testing, including who and when to test, how to obtain samples, what tests to run, and how to interpret and implement the results. Vice Admiral Bono, Dr. Lofy, and Dr. Fotinos join Dr. Tu for a Q&A discussion.

(Originally recorded on August 5, 2020)
