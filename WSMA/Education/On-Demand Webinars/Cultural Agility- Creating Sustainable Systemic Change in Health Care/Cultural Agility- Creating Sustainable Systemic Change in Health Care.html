Cultural Agility: Creating Sustainable Systemic Change in Health Care

Health Equity Mini-Series (Part 1)

Co-sponsored by the Washington Patient Safety Coalition and the WSMA

This Continuing Professional Development webinar will educate physicians and physician assistants on the current state of health inequity, describe how the systems that were created impact patient care, and outline strategies to create lasting change within health care organizations.

Guest Speaker

Bryon Lambert
Equity and Inclusion Program Director
Cross Cultural Health Care Program
