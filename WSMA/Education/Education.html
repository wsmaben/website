Education

As an ACCME accredited provider, the WSMA directly offers CME activities and other educational opportunities for physicians and physician assistants encompassing a broad range of subjects. The WSMA can also help you award CME credit for your activity—or help your organization gain accreditation. The WSMA works to ensure CME presented by our accredited providers meets the high standards adopted by the association and the ACCME.
