Exhibitors

To request an exhibit booth, please complete an online interest form.

Representative Registration

All exhibitor representatives must be registered for the meeting. Please provide the names of the exhibitors when you complete the “Exhibitor Booth Set-up Form” and a WSMA staff member will register them for the meeting. If you need to make changes after you complete the form, please contact jessica@wsma.org. Exhibit booths include two meeting registrations plus two lunch tickets for representatives. Exhibit representatives will not be allowed on the exhibit floor without a registration and a name badge.

Exhibitor Shipping and Storage

The Bellevue Westin has limited storage facilities. Shipments will only be accepted that arrive within 3 business days of the event. Neither the Bellevue Westin nor the WSMA are responsible for any loss or damage to materials sent prior to your event date. If you will be sending any large equipment or odd/oversize materials, you must coordinate in advance with the Bellevue Westin by contacting Kim Claeson, Complex Director of Event Planning and Operations at kim.claeson@marriott.com. Depending upon the weight and number of boxes, a drayage fee may be applied on a per box basis.

When shipping packages be sure to label as follows: The Westin Bellevue

  • No subletting of space is permitted without the prior written consent of the WSMA.
  • There is a $250 service fee on all cancellations received by September 1, 2021. No refunds for cancellations will be accepted after that date.
  • The WSMA and Bellevue Westin do not guarantee against theft, vandalism or otherwise.
  • It is expressly understood that exhibitors shall indemnify and hold harmless the WSMA and Bellevue Westin of all liability (damage or accident) that might ensue from any cause in connection with transfer, installation, maintenance or removal of exhibits, or in display of exhibits.
  • In the event of fire, strikes, or other uncontrollable circumstances, the contract for space will not be binding on either party.
  • It is expressly understood that the WSMA will not pay for special equipment, facilities, and services ordered by technical exhibitors.
  • Electrical and other apparatus must be operated so that the noise does not interfere with other exhibitors.
