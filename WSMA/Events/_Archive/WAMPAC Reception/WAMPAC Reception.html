2020 WAMPAC Reception Featuring Gov. Inslee

This year's WAMPAC Reception will be held via Zoom, beginning at 6:30 p.m. on Tuesday, Oct. 6. We're pleased to be joined by Gov. Jay Inslee , who will provide an update on the state's pandemic response and work in the health policy arena before taking questions from attendees. We'll also be awarding the WSMA Legislator of the Year to retiring Sen. Randi Becker (R-Olympia) for her leadership on telemedicine issues and her close work with the physician community during her legislative tenure. And we'll be joined by state House candidate Tracy Rushing, MD (D-White Salmon) to get her perspective on running for office as a physician during the pandemic.

Your contribution will help WAMPAC stay relevant and give us the resources we need to advocate on your behalf. After registering through one of the three registration tiers, you'll receive an email with more details on the event from Alex Wehinger, WSMA's associate director of legislative & political affairs, at alex@wsma.org .
