Opioids and Telehealth in a Pandemic


Join us Friday, May 15 from 1–2 p.m. for webinar on Opioids and Telehealth in a Pandemic

This webinar will help physicians and advanced practitioners care for their patients with opioid prescriptions for chronic pain via telemedicine during the pandemic. We will delve into: strategies to provide remote care for patients that you usually need to see in person, how to bill for telemedicine, regulatory changes to opioid prescribing and telemedicine policies, and the key features of the Better Prescribing, Better Treatment acute opioid reports.

About the Continuing Professional Development Webinar Series

Continuing Professional Development is the process of lifelong learning in which physicians acquire—through formal and informal activities—the medical and non-medical competencies they need to improve their ability to practice medicine and provide effective patient care. Engaging in CPD is a professional obligation and also a prerequisite for enhancing the quality of health care. The WSMA hosts this monthly CPD webinar series as part of our commitment to supporting physicians and PAs on their lifelong learning journey.
