Legislative Summit

Save the date! The 2022 WSMA Legislative Summit will take place on Feb. 8, 2022. More details to come as the date approaches.

About the Summit

Attend the WSMA Legislative Summit and help us turn WSMA policy into action.

At next year's Summit, join us, along with other top Washington state officials and health care leaders, as we kick off an action-packed day on the Hill dedicated to advancing priority issues for physicians, physician assistants, medical students, and patients.

  • Learn which legislative and budget items the WSMA believes will benefit most from your support during the state legislative session.
  • Receive information on how to meet with your legislators and effectively deliver our message.

We know your time is valuable: We'll provide lunch and we'll handle scheduling your (optional) personal meetings with your state legislative delegation in the afternoon.

This free event provides a unique opportunity to make your voice heard by the people who are deliberating the policies, laws, and regulations in Olympia that impact the practice of medicine in Washington.

Exclusive Premier Partner

Founded in 1981 under the auspices of the WSMA, Physicians Insurance A Mutual Company is the Northwest's leading medical professional liability insurance company, physician-owned and exclusively endorsed to this day by the WSMA. For over 30 years, Physicians Insurance has remained the stable, committed and local insurer for physicians, clinics, facilities and hospitals, providing risk-management consulting, continuing medical education and claims administration services to more than 7,000 members in Washington, Idaho, Oregon and Wyoming.

Supporter

Founded in 1992 by a network of community health centers (CHCs), Community Health Plan of Washington is a community-governed, not-for-profit health plan. CHPW serves approximately 250,000 Medicaid and Medicare members focusing on whole-person health and addressing health disparities.
