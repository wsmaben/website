Learn More - Resident or Fellow Physician or 1st Year Physician

To qualify for free membership, you need to be a resident or fellow physician in a training program in Washington state, or you need to be a physician starting your first year of medical practice in Washington state after recently completing your residency or fellowship training.

  • Click here to download an application (PDF). Complete and return the form to wsma@wsma.org to enroll in membership.

For assistance, email wsma@wsma.org or call 206.441.9762.
