Member Resource Center

Welcome to the Washington State Medical Association. Learn more about your membership and how to make the most of belonging to the largest physician professional association in Washington. And remember, personal support is simply a phone call or email away.

Get Started

Your Member Profile – Check that your information is up to date; the WSMA does not share personal information. Make edits using the pencil tool on your profile page.

Here's how:

  • Navigate to your profile via the membership tab. Check your contact information.
  • Verify or provide your professional ("office 1") and home addresses and select your preferred postal mailing address.
  • Add phone numbers and preferred email address.
  • Select your communication preferences.

How We Help You Practice Medicine – As the state's largest physician professional association—more than 11,000 members and growing—the WSMA is dedicated to helping you practice good medicine. Here's how.

 

Show Your WSMA Pride – Make a statement and display your digital member badge on your practice website, digital CV, LinkedIn page, and email signature. Click on the badge icon to download.


Stay Connected

Professional and Social Networking —Connecting you with your peers – WSMA's premier events offer opportunities to connect, network, and build community. By joining your peers and colleagues at our Annual Meeting of the House of Delegates, Legislative Summit in Olympia, Leadership Development Conference at Lake Chelan, or our Road Trips, you'll strengthen professional and personal connections.

OnDemand Info—Connecting you with resources – Take a spin around wsma.org to find the resources available to support you, your practice, and your patients. Discover and explore your WSMA by visiting these sections: Membership, Advocacy, Resources, Events, News & Publications, Services, and About WSMA.

Who Can Help—Connecting you with personal assistance – Your WSMA provides personal attention and assistance to members. Write us at wsma@wsma.org or call 206.441.9762 between 8:30 a.m.–5 p.m. We're here for you.


Be Informed

Physician Leadership — Equipping you to lead – Our top-notch leadership courses are best in class, equipping physician leaders to influence the business of medicine. We offer leadership development and professional training opportunities for physicians at all stages of their career.

Education & Professional Development — Enhancing your lifelong learning — The WSMA directly offers CME activities, education opportunities, and continuing professional development encompassing a broad range of subjects for physicians and physician assistants. Access real-time and on-demand webinars and seminars, plus a wide variety of other educational activities to enhance medical and non-medical competencies.

News & Publications—Informing you – As a member, we arm you with the news you need to know—and can't get anywhere else—via our various communications. Members exclusively receive WSMA Reports, our flagship magazine; Membership Memo, our e-newsletter; and Weekly Rounds, a personal message and reflection from WSMA's CEO.

Legal Resources—Providing on-demand information The WSMA online knowledge bank covers various topics including employment; payer or network sponsor contracting; medical and recreational marijuana; social media; and peer review. The Washington Physicians' Guide to Health Law 5th Edition—a $350 value—is a benefit exclusive to members.


Get Involved

Advocacy—Protecting you, your profession, and your patients – Our government affairs and policy teams are on the ground in Olympia every day advocating for your best interests. As the largest medical professional organization in the state, we not only raise up the physician voice, we also bring clout, credibility, and influence to legislative and regulatory actions.

Volunteer Opportunities—Engaging you – Members get involved across the board with the WSMA in activities that help shape the future of the profession. Interested in getting involved? Learn more about the many opportunities to serve with the WSMA for as much or as little time as you're able. If you don't see something that aligns with your interests, let us know how you'd like to get involved.


Use Your Benefits

WSMA's membership benefits can help you get ahead in medicine. Below is an introduction to the benefits of your membership. For a full list of benefits, visit our Member Benefits page.

Physicians Insurance A Mutual Company—Supporting you – Members can feel confident with Physicians Insurance, a trusted partner helping physicians, groups, and facilities manage risk and improve health care quality and patient safety. Most WSMA members insured with PI receive a $300 discount on their liability premium. PI offers other products to support you including:

  • Medical professional liability insurance
  • Life and disability insurance
  • Long-term care insurance
  • Medical, dental and vision insurance
  • Online CME
  • Risk management resource library

Clinical Quality Programs—Advancing quality improvement – Our quality and wellness initiatives support you in reducing burnout and administrative burden, ultimately helping you reconnect with joy in your profession as well as provide the best care for your patients.
