MACRA

MACRA—the Medicare Access and CHIP Reauthorization Act of 2015—will implement the most extensive collection of changes to Medicare payment methodologies since RBRVS was introduced in 1992.

To deepen your understanding and preparation, review the resources available below.

MACRA Webinars

Quality Payment Program Year 2 Overview Webinar

This webinar addresses the CMS 2018 Final Rule Changes regarding MACRA. The Medicare Access and CHIP Reauthorization Act of 2015 (MACRA) established the Quality Payment Program for eligible clinicians. The recently announced rule provides updates for the second and future years of the Quality Payment Program. This webinar, presented by Dr. Nancy Fisher, chief medical officer of CMS Region 10, and Lauri Tan, health insurance specialist, focuses on a comparison between calendar year 2017 and the proposed changes for calendar 2018. Originally presented Dec. 6, 2017.
