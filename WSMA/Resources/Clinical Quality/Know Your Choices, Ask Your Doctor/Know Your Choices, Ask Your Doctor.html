Know Your Choices, Ask Your Doctor

The WSMA's Know Your Choices, Ask Your Doctor initiative focuses on getting patients the right care, at the right place, at the right time.

Know Your Choices, Ask Your Doctor encompasses three WSMA clinical quality improvement campaigns: Choosing Wisely®, ER is for Emergencies and Honoring Choices® Pacific Northwest. Visit the individual campaign pages to explore the physician and patient resources available for each.

Know Your Choices, Ask Your Doctor was awarded the 2013 Profiles of Excellence Awards in the Communications category for outstanding achievement in medical society management by the American Association of Medical Society Executives.
