Influenza – For Physicians

During the 2020-21 influenza season, with the state still working to slow the spread of the novel respiratory virus SARS-CoV-2, it’s more important than ever for Washingtonians to get their flu shot.

On this page, find resources and information to help you and your practice or organization #FighttheFlu.

Flu toolkit for your practice

The Washington State Department of Health will launch its updated flu campaign in September. The final touches are being put on a partner toolkit—companion materials (In English and Spanish) for immunization partners to promote through their own communication channels. The WSMA will participate in a rapid review of the partner materials for equity or best practice issues and will publish them on this page and promote in our communication channels when ready.

DOH flu updates webinar

The Washington State Department of Health is presenting a free flu updates webinar on Sept. 23, 2020 from noon to 1 p.m. PDT. Topics will include:

  • Updated influenza epidemiology
  • Flu vaccine availability
  • Ordering flu vaccine in the Immunization Information System
  • Flu communication and health promotion resources

Register online. This activity has been approved for AMA PRA Category 1 Credit™. CE credit is available for non-physician health professionals.

Also, check out the DOH Immunization training web page for more resources and training opportunities.

Adult flu vaccine doses available

Through the aid of a CDC grant, the Washington State Department of Health will have additional adult doses for un- and under-insured individuals available later this fall. These adult doses will be distributed to physicians and providers participating in the state's adult vaccine program. To enroll, complete the provider agreement form located in Appendix A of the Adult Vaccine Program User Manual. Email WAAdultVaccines@doh.wa.gov for details.

Washington State Childhood Vaccine Program updates

As of early September, childhood flu vaccine is now available to order. Make sure your practice or organization is receiving important updates to the DOH vaccine ordering and delivery system. To subscriber or if you have questions, email the Vaccine Management team at WAChildhoodVaccines@doh.wa.gov or call at 360.236.2VAX.
