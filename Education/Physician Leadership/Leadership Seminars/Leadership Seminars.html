Leadership Seminars

Leadership Seminars are designed for graduates of the WSMA Physician Leadership Course (PLC). To provide in-depth exposure to the topics that were covered in the PLC course. There is an emphasis on skill development and practical application of leadership principles using role playing, demonstrations, and case studies. All seminars will be held over Zoom and will range between half-day to full day depending on the seminar topic.

This course is led by Edward A. Walker, MD, MHA, professor emeritus in the departments of psychiatry and behavioral sciences and health services at the University of Washington in Seattle.

Effective Governance Course**

Join us May 7, 2021, 8-5pm

**This course is open to alumni and non-alumni of our courses. You must be in a physician leadership role at your organization.

Now more than ever, physicians are being asked to serve on the board of their clinic, medical group or integrated system. But in order to be an effective board member it is important to understand the difference between a constituency-based board and a representative board, what's expected of you in terms of fiduciary responsibility, who you are expected to represent and how to be effective in communicating as a board member. Equipped with this knowledge, physicians can be even stronger leaders, and bring a powerful voice and perspective to any board.
