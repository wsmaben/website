HPV

The WSMA urges physicians to follow the Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices (ACIP) recommendation for routine administration of human papilloma virus (HPV) vaccine to 11- to 12-year-old girls. (Res A-1, A-06)

The WSMA supports state funding for the addition of HPV vaccine to the state childhood immunization program, with state support for an adequate supply of vaccine. (Res A-1, A-06)
