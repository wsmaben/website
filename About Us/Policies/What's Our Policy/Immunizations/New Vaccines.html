New Vaccines

The WSMA urges all physicians caring for children and adolescents to promote the new vaccines (tetanus, diphtheria and acellular pertussis [Tdap]; conjugated meningococcal; and HPV for girls) for children and adolescents and also use the immunization visits as opportunities to provide other important services, such as well child check-ups, risk assessment, and counseling, to this age group. (Res A-1, A-06)
