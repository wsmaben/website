Mandatory Health Care Worker Vaccination

The WSMA supports a statewide mandate by the state Board of Health requiring all health care workers with direct patient care responsibilities to show proof of annual influenza vaccination or appropriate documentation of a medical contraindication, religious exemption, or signed declination listing their reason for refusing immunization vaccination. (Res A-2, A-06)
