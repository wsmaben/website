Flu

The WSMA supports the Washington State Hospital Association's commitment to decrease morbidity from flu by encouraging immunization in their workforce and implementing infection prevention activities. (Res A-3, A-10)

The WSMA encourages all Washington physicians to receive appropriate and timely influenza vaccination and to encourage their office staff and patients likewise to receive such immunization. (Res A-3, A-10)
