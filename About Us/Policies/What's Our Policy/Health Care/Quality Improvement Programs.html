Quality Improvement Programs

The WSMA endorses statewide, physician-led quality improvement programs like COAP (Clinical Outcomes Assessment Program) and supports adequate funding through, but not limited to, collaborative public/private sources. (Res B-4, A-00) (Reaffirmed A-17)
