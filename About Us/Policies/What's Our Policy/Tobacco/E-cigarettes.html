E-cigarettes

The WSMA supports legislation that prohibits sales and marketing of e-cigarettes and similar devices to minors. (Res C-5, A-13)

The WSMA supports legislation that prohibits use of e-cigarettes and similar devices in public spaces. (Res C-5, A-13)
