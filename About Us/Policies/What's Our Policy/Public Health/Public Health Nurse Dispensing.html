Public Health Nurse Dispensing

The WSMA supports allowing registered nurses employed and directed by a local health department/district/clinic or facility under contract with a local health department/district to dispense a drug or device for purposes of prevention or treatment of a communicable disease or family planning. (Res C-2, A-13)
