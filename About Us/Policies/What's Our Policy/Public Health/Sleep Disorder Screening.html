Sleep Disorder Screening

The WSMA supports the creation of initiatives for increasing physician and patient awareness of screening and treatment of sleep disorders, especially for those who hold commercial drivers licenses, operate a motor vehicle, or operate other heavy machinery such as boats, cranes, aircraft, and lifts. (Res C-3, A-08)
