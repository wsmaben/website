Discoverability

The WSMA supports non-discoverability and exemption from freedom of information acts any data evaluating or measuring physician performance that is generated by a regularly constituted Quality Assurance Committee of a health care organization, regardless of whether the organization is a public or private entity. (BT 1/95) (Reaffirmed A-17)
