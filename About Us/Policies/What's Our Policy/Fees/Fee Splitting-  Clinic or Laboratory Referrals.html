Fee Splitting: Clinic or Laboratory Referrals

The WSMA believes that clinics or laboratories that compensate physicians based on the amount of work referred by the physician to the clinic or laboratory are engaged in fee splitting which is unethical. (JC 6.04-87) (Reaffirmed A-17)
