Washington Rural Health Association

The WSMA supports Washington Rural Health Association as an advocacy group for rural health care issues and concerns, and encourages its members to join and actively participate in the WRHA. (EC Rpt C, A-92; BT Rpt I, A-97; BT Rpt H, A-99; BT Rpt H, A-00; BT Rpt G, A-01) (Reaffirmed A-17)
