Access

The WSMA supports "First Steps", and encourages all obstetrical providers to actively provide maternity access to Medicaid beneficiaries, and assume a reasonable share of Medicaid patients in order to more equally distribute the workload. (EC Rpt H, A-90) (Reaffirmed A-17)
