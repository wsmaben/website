AMA Code of Ethics

The WSMA encourages and supports collaborative specialty development and review of any appropriateness criteria, practice guidelines, technical standards, and accreditation programs, and supports the AMA Code of Ethics as the guiding principle in the development of such programs. (Res C-14, A-05) (Reaffirmed A-17)
