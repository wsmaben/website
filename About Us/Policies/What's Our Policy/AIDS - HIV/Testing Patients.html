Testing Patients

The WSMA supports the development of effective and efficient statewide programs of provider-referral, assisted partner-referral, partner notification, and pre- and post-test counseling to maximize acceptance of HIV testing among persons at risk. (EC Rpt G, A-90) (Reaffirmed A-17)
