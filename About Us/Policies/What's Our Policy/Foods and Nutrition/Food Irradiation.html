Food Irradiation

The WSMA supports legislation classifying irradiation as a food process rather than as a food additive. (Res 1, C-84) (Reaffirmed A-17)

The WSMA supports the appropriate use of irradiation of food and encourages members to help educate their patients and their patients' relatives about this issue. (Res A-1, A-95) (Reaffirmed A-17)
