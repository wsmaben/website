What's Our Policy?

The WSMA Policy Compendium collects all policy as passed by the WSMA House of Delegates. The compendium was last updated November 2019.

Policies are listed below by subject. To search the compendium for a specific policy, click the search icon at the top of this page and enter a keyword(s).

NOTE: Pursuant to policy adopted at the 2014 House of Delegates meeting, the WSMA adopted in 2015 a "sunsetting" mechanism through which the WSMA may remove adopted policies from the WSMA Policy Compendium after a period of 10 years, unless the House of Delegates takes action to reaffirm or amend the policy. If the House of Delegates at any time takes action to either reaffirm or amend an existing policy, such action will reset the 10-year sunset period.
