Discrimination in Physician Employment

The WSMA endorses non-discrimination in physician employment, compensation, and advancement opportunities consistent with the Washington State Equal Pay Opportunity Act by publishing expectations for employers of physicians. (Res B-12, A-18)

The WSMA endorses the concept that employers should demonstrate fair and equitable compensation among employees. (Res B-12, A-18)
