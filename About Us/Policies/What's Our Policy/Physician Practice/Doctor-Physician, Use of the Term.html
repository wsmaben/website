Doctor/Physician, Use of the Term

The WSMA believes any practitioner who refers to him/herself as "doctor" or "physician" should be required to include their professional degree immediately after their name for the purpose of advertising, written correspondence, business cards, or any other printed professional representation. (Res C-8, A-03) (Reaffirmed A-17) (Reaffirmed A-18)
