Corporate Partners Program

The WSMA offers an exclusive opportunity for companies to increase visibility, create connections and enhance relationships with physicians, health care decision-makers and practice managers. As a corporate partner, your company can demonstrate its commitment to the medical community.

How we can help you

Through our corporate partner program, you can market your product or service by sponsoring and attending key WSMA events—the highly acclaimed Leadership Development Conference in the spring and the WSMA Annual Meeting in the fall—and via inclusion in our print and digital publications throughout your partnership year.

Become a partner and reach nearly 11,000 physician and physician assistant members from nearly all specialties and practice types from throughout Washington state, as well as numerous health care partners and associates. Details on partner benefits below.

Become a partner

To become a partner, download and submit the partnership agreement form with payment to the Washington State Medical Association, 2001 6th Ave., Suite 2700, Seattle, WA 98121. If paying by credit card, you may fax to 206.441.5863 or scan and email to Lauren Beverly at lauren@wsma.org. Questions? Contact Lauren via email or at 206.956.3622. WSMA reserves the right to approve all corporate partnerships.

Corporate Partner Benefits

Learn about the benefits that come with partnering with the state’s largest physician professional association.

All corporate partners will receive the following:

  • Recognition by the WSMA president at the Leadership Development Conference reception.
  • Recognition by the WSMA president at the Presidential Banquet at the WSMA Annual Meeting.
  • Your company logo and link to your website from the WSMA website and the event webpage.
  • 50-word company description on the event webpage.
  • Your company logo projected on screens in the meeting rooms before all major sessions.
  • Recognition in WSMA’s newsletters.
  • Nametag ribbons for your representative(s) identifying your level of support.
  • Complimentary subscription to WSMA Reports.

SILVER LEVEL – $5,000  |  All benefits listed above, plus:

  • Complimentary tabletop exhibit space at the WSMA Annual Meeting.
  • Three 1/6 page display ads in WSMA Reports.
  • Two vertical banner ads in Membership Memo.
  • Digital banner ad featured on WSMA website for one month.
  • Additional advertising available for purchase at discounted rates.
  • One complimentary ticket to the Presidential Banquet at the WSMA Annual Meeting.
  • One complimentary registration to the Leadership Development Conference.

GOLD LEVEL – $10,000  |  All benefits listed above, plus:

  • Complimentary tabletop exhibit space at the WSMA Annual Meeting.
  • Three 1/3 page display ads in WSMA Reports.
  • Six vertical banner ads in Membership Memo.
  • Digital banner ad featured on WSMA website for three months.
  • Additional advertising available for purchase at discounted rates.
  • A color flyer highlighting your company’s contribution distributed to meeting  participants during the WSMA Annual Meeting.
  • Two complimentary tickets to the Presidential Banquet at the WSMA Annual Meeting.
  • Two complimentary registrations to the Leadership Development Conference.

PLATINUM LEVEL – $15,000  |  (two available) All benefits listed above, plus:

  • Additional advertising available for purchase at discounted rates.

  • Your company logo printed on hotel card keys during the Leadership Development Conference and the WSMA Annual Meeting.

  • An opportunity for your company’s representative(s) to personally greet attendees at the door and provide attendees with complimentary drink tickets imprinted with your company logo at the Presidential Banquet during the WSMA Annual Meeting.
  • Four complimentary tickets to the Presidential Banquet at the WSMA Annual Meeting.
  • Four complimentary registrations to the Leadership Development Conference.
