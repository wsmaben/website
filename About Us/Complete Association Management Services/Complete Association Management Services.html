Complete Association Management Services

The WSMA understands the unique challenges of medical societies, including membership engagement, continuing education and legislative advocacy.

Through our Complete Association Management Services, the WSMA can provide medical organizations of all sizes (state and regional) with a professional and affordable solution to their management and administrative needs. Let us help you grow and thrive by assuming your administrative responsibilities so that your society's leadership can focus on members services, program development and policy.

How we can help

The WSMA's Complete Association Management Services 10-person staff provides groups with an exceptional level of professionalism and resources to draw from for all types of tasks. CAMS includes:

  • Financial management
  • Management of member records
  • Meeting data management
  • Meeting planning and coordination of CME activities
  • Development of exhibitor, support and grant opportunities
  • Website and communications
  • Leadership development

As an additional benefit, contracted groups receive limited advocacy, financial and legal services.

Societies Using CAMS

The WSMA's Complete Association Management Services today directs more than 20 specialty societies throughout the Northwest. In total, CAMS groups represent more than 7,000 member physicians, administrators or affiliate health care providers with gross revenues of more than $2.5 million. We plan, coordinate and staff 65 educational meetings each year and almost 90 board meetings. We work with more than 250 board members, close to 500 exhibitors, and complete more than 20 continuing medical education applications.
